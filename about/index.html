About

Headed by Charles Zhang, Joanna Zhang, and Rena Li, The Lisgar Math Contest Club brings new math concepts to the student population each week with fun and interactive lessons ranging from Combinatorics to Number Theory. Our meetings are held every Friday at lunch in room 316.
